Frequently Asked Questions

You can change or cancel your booking by visiting the "My Bookings" section in your dashboard. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the airline's policy and how close to departure your flight is.

For domestic flights, you typically need a valid government-issued photo ID. For international flights, you'll need a passport and possibly a visa depending on your destination. Check with the embassy or consulate of your destination country for specific requirements.

You can add baggage during the booking process or later through the airline's website using your booking reference. Adding baggage in advance is usually cheaper than paying at the airport.

If your flight is delayed or cancelled, the airline will typically rebook you on the next available flight at no extra cost. You may also be entitled to compensation depending on the circumstances and regulations in your region.

Seat selection can usually be done during booking or later through the airline's website. Some airlines charge for seat selection, while others offer it for free. You can also check in online 24 hours before departure to select available seats.

Policies & Information

Booking Policy

All bookings are subject to airline terms and conditions. Prices are subject to change until payment is completed. We recommend reviewing all booking details before confirming your purchase.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation terms vary by airline and ticket type. Refundable tickets can be cancelled for a full refund, while non-refundable tickets may incur cancellation fees or offer travel credits instead.

Privacy Policy

We protect your personal information and only share it with airlines and service providers necessary to complete your booking. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Payment Security

All payments are processed through secure, encrypted connections. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your financial information.
